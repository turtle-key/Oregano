Facebook Login setup

  1. Open Facebook Developers and create an App.
  2. Add product “Facebook Login” and choose “Web”.
  3. In Settings > Basic, copy the App ID and App Secret into the module.
  4. In Facebook Login > Settings add the Valid OAuth Redirect URL shown on the sociallogin module page.
  5. Put the App in Live mode after testing (you may need to verify the app).

Notes

  • Email permission is standard; users without public email will be assigned a surrogate email in your shop.
  • Ensure your site uses HTTPS.
